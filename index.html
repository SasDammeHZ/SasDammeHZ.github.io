Why I am interested in the ICT programme

First of all I want to say I do not have any background in coding or what not, and to be honest (for now) I don't even know a lot about computers and how they really work. But When I looked at the ICT programme I saw it was not required to have experience in the field, the programme is especially built to give you information about all sorts of basics in the ICT world. So this already made me excited and interested. It meant I could learn a lot of different skills and see which one I like to develop further in the future. Because of this I was not discouraged to join. I am also a person who likes to spend a lot of time on my computer, so doing a study which requires you to do a lot of things behind a monitor sounds like a good fit for me. The programme also offers a hand-on approach to ICT, we get the chance to do an internship and see what it's really like. I like the sound of that, because of this I can actually develop my skills through projects, and not just theory. From the assignments I had done on this programme, I liked what I did, perhaps a bit challenging but for now I am not discouraged by it because I feel satisfaction from what I am doing. Lastly the ICT programme offers a lot of job opportunities which is probably one of the biggest reasons why I joined the programme. I am someone who is not that confident about their future and the ICT programme gives a lot of security on this aspect. There are a lot of companies in need of people who are working in the ICT world. For example here is an article about the ICT shortages: ICT Shortages in the Netherlands

Why I think the ICT Programme is suited for me

I think the ICT programme is suited for me because I am keen to learn and interested in developing new skills. The programme structure aligns with my own goals; we are going to learn a lot of different skills and meet a lot of different people. I think I can adapt to people's needs in a project, at least to a certain extent, and that sounds like something that can be handy in the ICT world. And especially the ICT programme from HZ suits me because it is close to home, this way I don't get discouraged when traveling to school. This was a problem at my last study in Rotterdam, sometimes I had only 45 minutes of school, but for me to attend that class I had to travel for about 2 hours there and back, which greatly impacted my motivation. Lastly I think the ICT Programme is suited for me because it gives a lot of structure to my daily life, and that is something I want. I think I can benefit greatly from the structure the ICT programme provides.

List of personal benefits from joining the ICT programme:

  • Learning a lot of different ICT skills and improving those skills
  • I get satisfaction from the projects we are doing
  • Collaboration with lots of different people
  • It gives me the opportunity to get a steady job
  • It gives structure to my daily life
  • Close to home
