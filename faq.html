Frequently Asked Questions

How can you print a document from your laptop and scan a document and set it to your laptop at HZ?

First of all you need a HZ Student Identity card for printing and scanning documents. Then go to website hz.shop.canon.nl here you can browse the website and decide what you want to do.

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

You can Go to the HZ webshop and browse the website and decide what you want to buy and put into the shopping cart. Afterwards pay with your preferred payment method. You can also place your order via the Self Service Portal. For example: if you are looking for a gift pack, a specific book or a larger number of office supplies? Simply place your order via the Self Service Portal on MyHZ. That way, you will be helped quickly in the HZ Shop and your order will be processed with care. It is not possible to place your order via e-mail. Here is the e-mail of the shop if you are in need of it. e-mail: hzshop@hz.nl

How can you book a project space?

Go to the home page on Self Service Portal, click on reserve, select the correct category and search for the resource you want to reserve. If necessary, use the 'type' filter to switch certain items on/off in the overview. Read the loan conditions, check the reservation and click on next. Fill in the necessary information and click on confirm reservation.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

If you want to park at HZ Middelburg, then it is not possible to park directly at the HZ on Het Groene Woud. There is 1 special parking space available on the grounds for people with disabilities and/or chronic conditions. The car park on Kousteensedijk has a number of spaces for visitors, staff and students of Het Groene Woud. This car park is an 8-minute walk from the HZ. Upon showing your entry card, you can then get an exit card at the JRCZ service desk.
