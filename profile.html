About me

I am Sas van Damme

I am a 22 year old male and I was born and raised in the Netherlands. I lived my whole life in the little fishing village of Yerseke together with my parents and sister. The picture on the right is younger me (right) with my sister (left) and (middle) my father. At the moment I work a part-time job at G&B in Yerseke. Before I followed the HBO-ICT programme at HZ, I was studying Philosophy, in Rotterdam, at the Erasmus. I fully completed my first year of philosophy, but the second year was not a fun experience for me and I decided I needed a chance in my life, so now I am here studying at Middelburg!

My hobbies are not that diverse and I am especially more into the nerdy things. I like listening to music and I am always interested in hearing about new music. I love to watch movies and tv shows, I don't care if it is sci-fi, horror or romance. I enjoy all of the genres! I like to play video games, I especially play games like Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice, Hollow Knight, Terraria and Breath of the Wild. I am also an avid card game fan, I like to play Magic: The Gathering and I collect Pokémon cards.
