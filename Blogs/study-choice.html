Study Choice

When I decided I needed to do something other than philosophy I personally talked to student dean of the university of the Erasmus, about what kind of other study suits me. And that is one of the reasons why I eventually ended up at HBO-ICT. After I finish the HBO-ICT course I will most likely see myself looking for a job in the ICT world. The most obvious is helping customers and companies with ICT related assignments/problems. In addition, my personal interest lies in starting small projects myself and working on them at my own pace, so perhaps I will work on these kinds of things.
